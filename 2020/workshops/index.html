Info

Register for free admission by January 30.

Processing Community Day NYC is Saturday February 8, 2019 at The New School in New York.

Processing Community Day NYC is an independent all volunteer-organized event in cooperation with the Processing Foundation and hundreds of other locally-organized nodes around the world. Processing Community Day NYC 2019 is supported by the Department of Education Computer Science for All (CS4All). The New School is provided hosting support, with additional funding from Code + Liberal Arts at Eugene Lang.
